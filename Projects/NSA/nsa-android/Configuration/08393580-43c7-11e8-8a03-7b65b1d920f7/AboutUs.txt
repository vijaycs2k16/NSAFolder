<b>Alangad Jama-ath Public School</b> was started in the year 1996. It is situated at Karumalloor in a serene and peaceful atmosphere, far from the din and bustle of the noisy city.</br></br>It is conveniently located near all the basic amenities and easily accessible from various parts of Aluva and Paravur areas. The school can boast of a total constructed area of over 14,000 sq.mts. It has fully equipped labs and a well stocked library catering to the requirements of the students.</br></br>The spotrs complex consists of a magnificent playground, with all facilities. The school has been upgraded to Senior Secondary on 01.03.2011.</br></br>
